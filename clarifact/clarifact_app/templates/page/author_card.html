2. CHECK THE AUTHOR

Check the author’s bio and do a quick search on them. Are they credible to write about their story? Are they real? Are they experienced?
Trust the author before you trust the news!

01

Does the News article has an author or not?

Fake news articles often don't include author names. If included, check the author's bio and do a quick search on them.

02

Do a quick check on the author

Check the author’s byline to find out what else they have written and where their stories have been published.

03

What qualification does the author have?

If they have the professional backgrounds that they are writing about, they have met the minimum requirement for credibility.
