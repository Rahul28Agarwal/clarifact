4. CHECK FOR BIASES

What makes you think fake news is real news? Why can’t facts change our mind? Our brains contain something called confirmation bias that is defined as a limitation in our thinking which can cause flaws in our judgement. Confirmation bias leads us to believe what we want to believe and ignore or minimize facts that threaten our beliefs.

01

Recognize

recognize that you have this bias in the first place, We all do!

02

Consider

Consider that you may not really understand what you think you understand, and think about the other side of your perspective.

03

Research

Research the opposing viewpoint, you might realize that your understanding of where they’re coming from is a bit too shallow.
